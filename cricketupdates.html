IPL 2022 Information..!!

IPL 2022 New Teams:

The two new teams that have been added to the IPL setup are the Lucknow and the Ahmedabad teams. While the Lucknow franchise was bought by the RPSG Group in an auction for more than INR 7000 Crores, the Ahmedabad franchise has been bought by a private CVC Capital company for almost about INR 6000 Crores.
