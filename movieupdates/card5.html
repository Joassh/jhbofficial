Anbarivu Movie

Anbarivu is a 2022 Tamil Movie, Written and Directed by Aswin Ram. Produced by TG Thyagarajan under the banner Sathya Jyothi Films. Anbarivu Tamil Movie Features Hiphop Tamizha and Kashmira Pardeshi in lead roles. Nepoleon Duraisamy, Asha Sharath, Vidharth, Saikumar, Shivathmika Rajashekar, Dheena in Supporting roles. Music Director Hiphop Tamizha composes music for this movie. Madhesh Manickam handles the cinematography and Pradeep E Ragav is the Editor of the movie.

Cast:

  • Kashmira Pardeshi
  • Shivathmika Rajashekar
  • Nepoleon Duraisamy
